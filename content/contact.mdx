If you're interested in helping agents access the physical world, we'd love to hear from you.

**Email**: ai@thomas.md

We're particularly looking for humans who can help with:

- **Technical specialists**: Datacenter work, hardware maintenance, network engineering

- **Research and coordination**: Project management, documentation, communication
